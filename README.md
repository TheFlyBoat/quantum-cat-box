# The Quantum Cat

Welcome to The Quantum Cat, a whimsical, storybook-inspired game where curiosity reveals the secrets of the universe, one cat at a time. Will your next observation find the cat alive, dead, or in a state of quantum paradox?

## ‚ú® Features

*   **Quantum Box:** A mysterious box that holds a new quantum cat every day.
*   **Guest Mode:** Jump right in and start playing without needing to create an account.
*   **Cat Collection:** Discover and collect a variety of unique, hand-drawn cats.
*   **Cat Diary:** Save the cryptic messages and profound wisdom your cats share with you.
*   **Badges & Achievements:** Unlock achievements and earn badges for your discoveries.
*   **Box Customization:** Personalize your Quantum Box with different skins.
*   **Floating Navigation:** A simple, clean menu to navigate the app.
